For the last two years and several months, all people have been suffering from COVID-19 itself and the related effects of the disease. The first time COVID-19 occurred, the Korean government and citizens were confused about finding the most effective way to trace and prevent the disease's spread. The earliest infected people even got intruded on their privacy for the public interest. Moreover, many healthcare professionals and media introduced how different COVID-19 symptoms from the flu are. However, now, action for COVID-19 has faced a new condition. Although those two diseases still have some differences, experts encourage people to spend less time worrying about the differences and more time healing (Covid Clinic, 2022). Moreover, they also say that two diseases are extremely manageable except for people who are not reasonably healthy, just by eating and sleeping well (Covid Clinic, 2022). From this point of view, it would be natural to get less aggressive regarding regulation for COVID-19. Thus, this essay will address how the Korean government should manage variants of COVID-19 and future pandemics.
