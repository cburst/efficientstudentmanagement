On the heels of President-elect Donald Trump's successful re-election bid, including in Wisconsin, Vice President Kamala Harris delivered a concession speech Wednesday afternoon. Harris addressed the nation from Howard University, her alma mater in Washington, D.C. She was also scheduled to speak from Howard Tuesday night at her would-be victory party, but her remarks were cancelled as the election began to swing in Trump's favor.
